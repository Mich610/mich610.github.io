Skills: - Interpersonal; ie – listening, teamwork, responsibility. - Research, communication and technical; - ie ability to use financial planning software. Experience: - Although the ad doesn’t specify years of experience required, it does they require an adviser that meets all FASEA education requirements. Skills: I believe I have all relevant skills required and will continue to build on these skills through further training and practice. Ideally I will work myself up at my current workplace and eventually take the role of Financial Adviser.
